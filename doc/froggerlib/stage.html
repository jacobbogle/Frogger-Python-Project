Stage¶

A Stage object represents the “safe zone” for the frog in the game window. A stage should be placed at the bottom of the window where the frog starts the game as well as in the middle of the window between the road lanes and the water lanes.

class Stage(x, y, w, h)¶
x¶

The position of the stage in the x dimension; its left edge.

y¶

The position of the stage in the y dimension; its top edge.

w¶

The width of the stage.

h¶

The height of the stage.
