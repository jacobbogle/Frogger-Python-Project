Road
-----

A ``Road`` object represents the lanes in the bottom half of the window where
cars will move. After the bottom stage, there should be at least four road lanes
in the game window.

.. py:class:: Road(x,y,w,h)

   .. py:attribute:: x

       The position of the road in the x dimension; its left edge.

       :type x: int or float

   .. py:attribute:: y

       The position of the road in the y dimension; its top edge.

       :type y: int or float

   .. py:attribute:: w

       The width of the road.

       :type w: int or float

   .. py:attribute:: h

       The height of the road.

       :type h: int or float
